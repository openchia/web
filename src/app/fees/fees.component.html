Base fee

Currently we charge 1% fee. If a reward of 1.75 XCH is found, only 1% of that, 0.0175 will be kept to the pool as a fee.
The remaining 1.7325 XCH will be distributed to pool farmers.

Length of Stay Discount

Farmers that stay with us for longer periods will gradually get discounts over the base fee.

Up to 0.15% of discount will be subtracted from base fee in a period of up to 18 months (548 days), increased weekly.

Example: After 6 months (180 days) you will get a discount of 0.0479%, effective pool fee of 0.9520%.

Leaving the pool for more than 12 hours will reset the discount! Farm being offline or stop sending partials will NOT reset the discount.

Size Discount

Farmers can get discount based on their farm size:

Above 200TiB: 0.05%

Above 500TiB: 0.1%

Above 1PiB: 0.15%

Maximum Discount

The combination of discounts will not exceed 0.25%!


We use PPLNS (Pay Per Last N Shares) to distribute rewards.
When a block is won, each farmer will receive the share correspondent of the last 24 hours of partials sent to the pool.

Payouts will happen within 5 minutes of the farmed block.
These terms are subjected to change and will be communicated 1 week prior to the change in this website and on Discord channel.
